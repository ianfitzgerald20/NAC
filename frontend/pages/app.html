About Ray

Ray Fitzgerald is a Data Scientist and Quantum Physicist at Booz Allen Hamilton, where he works on Post-Quantum Cryptography and national security applications of quantum computing. A Norfolk, Virginia native, Ray graduated from Norfolk Academy in 2019 and went on to Harvard University, where he studied Physics — driven by a fascination with how fundamental science translates into real-world impact.

"Norfolk Academy gave me the intellectual curiosity and discipline to tackle genuinely hard problems. The culture of the school pushes you to think rigorously and take ownership of your own path — that's exactly the mindset you need in a field like quantum computing." Ray is one of the youngest alumni on AcademyConnect and offers a direct window into STEM careers, defense tech, and breaking into a field that barely existed when he was a student.
